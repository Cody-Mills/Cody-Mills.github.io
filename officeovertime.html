OFFICE OVERTIME - GAME DESCRIPTION




Office Overtime is a game where you have to defend an office worker working overtime from hoards of nightmarish monsters. Use 3 weapons to defeat different enemies and protect the worker's sanity. You can play the game on Itch.io here.




ENGINE OF CHOICE

I built this game originally in the Unity Engine for a University Project but after gaining more coding experience I decided to remake the game in my original vision in the Godot Engine.
