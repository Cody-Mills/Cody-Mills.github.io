ROOFTOP GUNNING - GAME DESCRIPTION


1 / 3
2 / 3
3 / 3

You are a living Tank on the run from a group out to kill you. Escape them and defend yourself whilst traversing the high roofs of city skyscrapers whilst trying to not die. Use a range of unique weapons to take down your enemies and use your high mobility dash & time dilation abilities to come out ontop.

You can play the game on Itch.io here.




TECHNICAL INFORMATION

This game was built in Unreal Engine 5.3 as a University project. This was my first ever game I made in any engine and was my first time trying Unreal Engine. The development process of this game was extremely fun but also challanging, with this being my first game it was tough balancing the learning aspect with the ambition of wanting to do alot. I am mostly proud of the general look of the level, especially the begining, I knew I had no experience in level design so I used the assets in a way that made the level design easier whilst making it still look good by using the top of buildings.

This project was developed using no AI. All assets were provided to me from Staffordshire University.
Developed from January 10th 2023 - April 13th 2023.





Overall I think this project whilst being simple and having alot of help from University staff, I think this is a decently high quality first game and I am proud of it. If I were to remake this game now (circa 2025) I would choose to make UI for both the players dash ability which would show it's cooldown & I would have a visual indicator that better shows when you are using a new weapon.
