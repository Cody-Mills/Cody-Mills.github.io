BUG BOX STUDIOS - DESCRIPTION


Box Bug Studios is a 26-person team created as part of the Collaborative Games Development module at the University of Staffordshire. Box Bug Studios did a full start-to-finish production of the game "Boxed Up" over span of 8 Weeks. The Studio was comprised of:
  • 5 - Programmers
  • 8 - 3D Artists
  • 5 - Concept Artists
  • 5 - Designers
  • 1 - VFX Designers
  • 1 - Audio Engineer
  • 1 - Animator
