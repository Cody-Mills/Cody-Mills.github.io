Witching Hour is a VR Horror experience where you venture a hedge maze located in a graveyard where you experience paranormal activity. You explore the graveyard and maze using a flashlight and walkie-talkie as your only light and communication as you attempt to make it to the other side.

You can try the experience here (VR HEADSET REQUIRED & MATURE CONTENT WARNING)




TECHNICAL INFORMATION

I built this experience using the Godot Game Engine using the XR Tools addon. Despite the Godot VR community being very small I found this I built this game originally in the Unity Engine for a University Project but after gaining more coding experience I decided to remake the game in my original vision in the Godot Engine. The Game was developed entirely by myself with no AI used or assets from outside sources.
