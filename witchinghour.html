Witching Hour is a VR Horror experience where you venture a hedge maze located in a graveyard where you experience paranormal activity. You explore the graveyard and maze using a flashlight and walkie-talkie as your only light and communication as you attempt to make it to the other side.

You can try the experience here (VR HEADSET REQUIRED & MATURE CONTENT WARNING)



TECHNICAL INFORMATION

I built this experience using the Godot Game Engine using the XR Tools addon. Despite the Godot VR community being very small I found this addon very well developed & I am glad to have contributed to the growing number of XR projects in Godot. This was my first time ever creating anything for VR so there was alot of unique challanges I faced during development. A unique challange of VR is that it can be hard to predict where the players attention is at anytime, this one of the major issues I had to overcome during development but I feel I did a good job of guiding the player through the goal of the game as well as the most interesting content without ruining immersion.


This project was developed using no AI. I used assets that were available for public use with the original sources all cited on the Itch.io page.
Developed from 10th March 2025 - 13th May 2025.
