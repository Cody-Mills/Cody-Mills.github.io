ABOUT ME

My name is Cody Mills and i'm a Second Year Computer Games Programming student at the University of Staffordshire. I have a huge passion for all type media but especially video games. Some of my favourite video games are 'Dark Souls', 'Outer Wilds' & 'Psychonauts'. I love open-source software and customisation with my game engine of personal choice being Godot. I work on alot of personal projects in my own time but I believe that I also fit well within a collabrative team.

I have worked with large development teams like Box Bug Studios & smaller teams like Big Hudsons Studios. For most of my Projects I make games for my own Studio New Groove Studios.
